Shoulder To Cry

By Harsh Patel | Published: October 15, 2024

To YOU,
If you’ve ever lost someone.
Maybe they’re not here, or maybe they’re too far.
Well this one comes straight from a castle, deep in my heart.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

They say - It's the order of the world
Is it really some kind of order?
Because it shook the world around me
Like I thought it would never

Broke my spirit from its core
It got so dark, I feel blind
Left a mark, this emptiness
Can nature really be this unkind?

Can someone stop me from crying?
Can this suffering come to a stop?
The cheeks complain it's too heavy
But politely I asked - just one more drop?

I’m not asking for much support
I just need a shoulder to cry
Well, someone somewhere said
You can ALWAYS have mine.
