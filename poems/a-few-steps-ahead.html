A Few Steps Ahead

By Harsh Patel | Published: August 17, 2025

You can run on water
You can sleep on clouds
If seeking for answers
Must confront your doubts

So in the shadows
And in the spotlight
Keep fueling that fire
Keep feeding that grind!

Because...
All that you want; all that you dreamt
Lie a few steps ahead of where you think its kept.

Brew up - your intent
Like the rising sun
Orange-Fresh-Limit-less
Beyond the horizon.

Never stop trying, your
efforts know no end
Like the waves of the ocean
Keep crashing on the sand.

All that you want; all that you dreamt
Lie a few steps ahead of where you think its kept.

Warm hugs; fluff blankets
Made up for the crowd
Your peace, rest & comfort
Lay beneath your shroud

So don't give up
And don't tap out
Wanna be a champion?
Gotta fight your bout.

All that you want; all that you dreamt
Lie a few steps ahead of where you think its kept.
