What Will Be

By Johan Yapo | Published: September 12, 2025

I knew why it happened, why this maze I'm in come out of the blue
dreams within dreams in which many things were still true
I don't want to head outside still sitting at home with you,
but I'm sure thats wrong.

Look to my friend, to figure out what to do,
They gave me a mask, said I made it for you.
I think their mothers must have told them to,
So, let's make it a party for two.

Damn I've been here too long
Sunken in place staring life through a screen unable to get out and unsure what to do
Paused in time and space waiting for what will be to be

Now I'm learning all the important stuff,
What do I truly value?
Who I want to share my life with?
I've got to let go, get on my feet
I've got this.

Damn, I've been here too long
Warped into the screen, in another dimension that I've been dreaming of
It's clear now, that I must keep moving on
I was at the end of every tether waiting for what will be to be.
