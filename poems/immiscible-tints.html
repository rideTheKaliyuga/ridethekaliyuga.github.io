Immiscible Tints

By Harsh Patel | Published: December 23, 2023

A hue of blue, with a hint of haze
That's her personality; in its face
Am I colorblind? Can’t I see that she's
A color that doesn't really match my shade

We compliment each other; side by side
Look good together, at least in my blind eyes.
Two colors - that no one has ever tried
To combine before, and it makes sense why!

It’s one thing, to be next to each other
And another, to merge into one another
Only a true artist could impossibly imagine
To make a masterpiece from these colors.

Let’s close our eyes & dream of the light
Of that painting we could be, in all it’s might
Breaks my heart to open up my eyes
Until she tells me; I can rest them tonight.
